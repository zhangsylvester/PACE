北京大学代数组合暑期科研

General Information

代数组合是一个用组合模型研究代数结构，用代数方法解决组合问题的重要研究领域，尤其与代数几何和表示论关系紧密。其具体研究方向包含舒伯特计数演算、李群李代数的表示论、热带几何、拟阵、考科斯特群、多面体等。为激发优秀本科生科研兴趣，培养代数组合领域拔尖人才，北京国际数学研究中心将自2023年开始举办代数组合本科生暑期科研项目（PKU Algebra and Combinatorics Experience, PACE）。该活动将为有志于数学研究的优秀本科生提供浸入式的短期科研体验和长期的学习交流平台。

The study of algebraic combinatorics is a significant research area that employs combinatorial models to investigate algebraic structures and utilizes algebraic methods to solve combinatorial problems. It is particularly closely related to algebraic geometry and representation theory. Specific research directions include Schubert calculus, representation theory of Lie groups and Lie algebras, tropical geometry, quasisymmetric functions, Coxeter groups, polytopes, and more.

In order to inspire research interest among outstanding undergraduate students and cultivate top talents in the field of algebraic combinatorics, the Beijing International Mathematical Research Center will host the Algebra and Combinatorics Experience for Undergraduates (PACE) starting from 2023. This activity aims to provide immersive short-term research experiences and a long-term platform for learning and exchange for undergraduate students aspiring to engage in mathematical research.

The REU adopts a collaborative model, with over ten experts in the field providing problems for students to choose from freely and engage in group discussions. At the conclusion of the projects, each group shall publicly present their findings. Projects with significant achievements are encouraged to promptly submit their results to academic journals for publication.

本科研活动采取合作讨论的研究模式，由国内外近十名领域专家供题，学生自由选取感兴趣的项目进行小组合作讨论。项目结束时，每个课题小组需进行公开汇报展示；成果显著的课题小组可随即将成果投稿至学术期刊以及学术会议。
